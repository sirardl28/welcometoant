Animals of the Ocean!

Dolphins are intelligent marine mammals known for their playful behavior and complex communication skills. They often travel in pods and can be found in oceans around the world.

Octopuses have soft bodies and eight arms, and they can change color and texture to blend into their surroundings. They're known for their problem-solving abilities and escaping from enclosures.

Sea turtles are ancient reptiles that spend most of their lives in the ocean, only coming ashore to lay eggs. They can live for several decades and travel thousands of miles during migration.

Clownfish are small, brightly colored fish that live among sea anemones for protection. They have a mutualistic relationship with the anemone, helping keep it clean while staying safe from predators.
